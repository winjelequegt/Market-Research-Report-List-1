<p><ul><li>Dry Core</li><li>Jelly-filled Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telecom copper cable market is primarily divided into two types: dry core and jelly-filled core cables. Dry core cables utilize a non-gel compound that prevents water ingress, making them suitable for indoor applications and environments where moisture is minimal. In contrast, jelly-filled core cables are filled with a jelly-like substance that provides superior protection against moisture, making them ideal for outdoor use and humid conditions. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring reliable telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
