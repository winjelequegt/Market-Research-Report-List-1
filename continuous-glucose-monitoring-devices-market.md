<p><p>The Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) Devices market comprises three primary types: sensors, transmitters, and receivers, as well as insulin pumps. Sensors measure glucose levels in interstitial fluid, providing real-time data to users. Transmitters wirelessly send this data to receivers or compatible apps for easy monitoring. Receivers display glucose trends and alerts. Insulin pumps are integrated systems that deliver insulin based on glucose readings, enhancing diabetes management by allowing for continuous adjustments and more precise control of blood glucose levels.</p></p>
