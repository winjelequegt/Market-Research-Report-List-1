Connectors with a hemostatic valve help prevent backflow and manage airflow during procedures, enhancing safety and efficiency. In contrast, connectors without a hemostatic valve are simpler, often used in situations where backflow is less of a concern. The 'others' category includes specialized connectors that may incorporate unique features for specific medical applications, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
