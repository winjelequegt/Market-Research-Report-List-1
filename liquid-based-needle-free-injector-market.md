<p><ul><li>Jet</li><li>Spring</li><li>Laser</li><li>Vibration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid-based needle-free injector market is categorized into four main types: jet, spring, laser, and vibration injectors. Jet injectors use high-pressure gas to create a narrow stream of liquid that penetrates the skin, while spring injectors rely on a spring mechanism to deliver doses quickly. Laser injectors utilize focused laser technology to create micro-channels in the skin for drug delivery, and vibration injectors employ ultrasound or vibration to enhance permeability, facilitating injections without needles. Each type offers unique advantages for efficient and pain-free administration of medications.</p></p>
