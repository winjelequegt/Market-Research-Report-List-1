<p><strong>The Palm Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Scanning</li><li>Vein Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The palm scanner market is primarily categorized into two types: optical scanning and vein scanning. Optical scanning utilizes cameras to capture the unique patterns of ridges and valleys on the palm's surface, offering a fast and cost-effective identification method. In contrast, vein scanning employs near-infrared light to detect the unique vein patterns beneath the skin, providing a more secure and accurate form of biometric identification. Both technologies enhance security and improve user convenience in various applications like access control and payment systems.</p></p>
