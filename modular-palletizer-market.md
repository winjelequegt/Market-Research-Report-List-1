<p><p>The modular palletizer market is categorized into types based on their loading mechanisms: Inline Infeed, Sweep Infeed, and Others. Inline Infeed systems streamline the palletizing process by aligning products in a straight line for direct entry into the palletizer. Sweep Infeed systems utilize a sweeping motion to collect and arrange products efficiently. Other variants may include combinations of these methods or specialized designs tailored for specific applications, enhancing flexibility and productivity in automated packaging environments.</p></p>
