<p><ul><li>Single Shuttle Racking</li><li>Sub Shuttle Racking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shuttle racking systems enhance warehouse efficiency by optimizing space and streamlining operations. Single shuttle racking involves a single shuttle that moves along the rack's aisle, transporting pallets to and from storage locations, ideal for high-density storage of similar products. In contrast, sub shuttle racking features multiple shuttles that operate within a single aisle, allowing for greater flexibility and faster access to items. Both systems are designed to maximize storage capacity and improve inventory management in warehouses.</p></p>
