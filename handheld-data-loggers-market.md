<p><p>The handheld data loggers market is categorized into three types based on channel capacity. Single channel loggers capture data from one sensor, ideal for basic applications like temperature monitoring. Dual channel loggers can record from two sensors simultaneously, allowing for comparative analysis, such as temperature and humidity. Multi channel loggers accommodate multiple sensors, enabling intricate monitoring across various parameters, suitable for complex experiments or systems. These varied logger types meet diverse data collection needs across industries.</p></p>
