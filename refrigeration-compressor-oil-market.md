In contrast, synthetic oil is chemically engineered, providing superior thermal stability, lower volatility, and enhanced performance at extreme temperatures. This makes it suitable for modern, high-efficiency systems. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring optimal compressor function and longevity in refrigeration systems.</p></p>
