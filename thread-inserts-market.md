<p><p>Thread inserts are components used to reinforce or create threads in materials, enhancing their durability and functionality. The market can be divided into two main types: tanged thread inserts and tangless thread inserts. Tanged thread inserts have tabs that facilitate insertion and positioning within the host material, ensuring stability during assembly. In contrast, tangless thread inserts are designed for applications that require a smoother finish and reduced installation complexity, often used in scenarios where space is limited or aesthetics are crucial.</p></p>
