<p><p>The Bundle Branching Fiber-optic Patch Cord market comprises two main types: Single Mode Bundle Patch Cord and Multi-mode Bundle Patch Cord. Single Mode Bundle Patch Cords are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light wave to ensure minimal signal loss over extended ranges, making them ideal for high-bandwidth applications. In contrast, Multi-mode Bundle Patch Cords support multiple light paths, facilitating data transmission over shorter distances with greater capacity, commonly used in local area networks and data centers. Both types cater to diverse connectivity needs in modern communication systems.</p></p>
