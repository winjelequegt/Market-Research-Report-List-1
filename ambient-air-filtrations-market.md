<p><p>The Ambient Air Filtration market encompasses various technologies aimed at improving indoor air quality. HEPA filters capture small particles effectively, while activated carbon absorbs odors and volatile organic compounds. Static electricity systems trap airborne particles using charged plates. Ionizers release negative ions to attract and remove pollutants, whereas ozone generators produce ozone to neutralize odors and kill bacteria. Additional methods include UV light filtration and electrostatic precipitators. Each technology serves distinct air purification needs based on specific contaminants and environments.</p></p>
