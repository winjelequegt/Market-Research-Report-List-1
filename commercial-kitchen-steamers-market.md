<p><p>The commercial kitchen steamers market comprises primarily two types: gas steamers and electric steamers. Gas steamers utilize natural gas or propane for heating, providing rapid cooking and high performance, ideal for busy kitchens. They offer flexibility in installation and operation. Electric steamers, on the other hand, rely on electricity for heating, offering precise temperature control and ease of use. They are often compact and suitable for locations where gas supply is limited, making both types essential for diverse culinary needs.</p></p>
