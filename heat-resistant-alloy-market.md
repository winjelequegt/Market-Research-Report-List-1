<p><p>The heat-resistant alloy market can be categorized into three main types: nickel-based, iron-based, and cobalt-based alloys. Nickel-based alloys are known for their exceptional high-temperature strength and resistance to oxidation, making them suitable for aerospace and gas turbine applications. Iron-based alloys offer good thermal stability and are commonly used in automotive and industrial sectors. Cobalt-based alloys provide superior wear resistance and are often utilized in applications requiring extreme corrosion resistance, like in gas turbines and chemical processing environments.</p></p>
