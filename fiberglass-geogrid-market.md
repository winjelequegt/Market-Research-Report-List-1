<p><p>The fiberglass geogrid market is categorized into two main types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension models. Biaxial tension geogrids are designed to provide reinforcement in both horizontal and vertical directions, making them suitable for applications such as roadways and base stabilization. In contrast, uniaxial tension geogrids are engineered to carry loads in one direction, often used in retaining walls and slope stabilization projects. Both types enhance soil stability and increase load-bearing capacity in various civil engineering applications.</p></p>
