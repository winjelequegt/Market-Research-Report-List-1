<p><p>The Disposable Soft Tissue Dissection Device market comprises two main types: bipolar and monopolar devices. Bipolar devices utilize two electrodes that simultaneously deliver energy, allowing for precise cutting and coagulation with reduced thermal spread, making them ideal for delicate tissues. In contrast, monopolar devices use a single electrode to generate a high-frequency current, allowing for efficient cutting and coagulation but potentially affecting surrounding tissues more significantly. Each type serves distinct surgical needs, influencing their adoption in various medical procedures.</p></p>
