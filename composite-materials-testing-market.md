<p><p>Composite materials testing encompasses various methods to ensure their performance and safety. Physical testing evaluates properties like strength, toughness, and durability under mechanical stress, temperature variations, and environmental conditions. It includes techniques such as tensile testing and impact resistance assessment. Chemical testing focuses on the material's resistance to corrosion, degradation, and chemical interactions, assessing factors like solvent compatibility and thermal stability. Both testing types are crucial for verifying the reliability and application suitability of composite materials in industries like aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
