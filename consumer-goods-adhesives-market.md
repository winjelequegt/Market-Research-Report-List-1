<p><ul><li>One-component Adhesive</li><li>Two-component Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The consumer goods adhesives market comprises two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to air or heat, offering convenience and ease of application for tasks like bonding materials in crafts or home repairs. In contrast, two-component adhesives require the mixing of a resin and hardener before application, providing superior strength and durability, making them ideal for demanding applications such as automotive or heavy-duty household projects.</p></p>
