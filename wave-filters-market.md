<p><ul><li>Active Filters</li><li>Passive Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wave filters market is categorized into active and passive filters. Active filters utilize external power sources and electronic components like operational amplifiers to enhance signal processing, offering better performance and flexibility. In contrast, passive filters rely on passive components such as resistors, capacitors, and inductors, requiring no external power. They are simpler and cost-effective but may have limited performance. Both types serve critical functions in various applications, including telecommunications, audio processing, and signal conditioning.</p></p>
