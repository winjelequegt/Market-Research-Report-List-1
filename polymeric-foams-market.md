<p><ul><li>Closed Cell Foams</li><li>Open Cell Foams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymeric foams market is primarily divided into two main types: closed cell and open cell foams. Closed cell foams have distinct cells that are sealed, making them buoyant, moisture-resistant, and providing excellent insulation, commonly used in refrigeration and construction. In contrast, open cell foams feature interconnected cells that allow air and moisture to flow freely, resulting in greater flexibility and sound absorption, making them ideal for applications like upholstery and acoustic insulation. Both types serve diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
