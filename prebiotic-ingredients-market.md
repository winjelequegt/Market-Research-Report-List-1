<p><strong>The Prebiotic Ingredients Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inulin</li><li>Polydextrose</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prebiotic ingredients market consists of various types, notably inulin and polydextrose. Inulin, a natural carbohydrate derived from chicory root, promotes gut health by enhancing beneficial bacteria growth. It is commonly used in food products as a fiber source. Polydextrose, a synthetic polymer of glucose and sorbitol, serves as a low-calorie bulking agent while also providing prebiotic benefits. Both ingredients are increasingly popular in functional foods and dietary supplements, supporting digestive wellness and overall health.</p></p>
