<p><p>The automotive PCB relays market encompasses various types based on power handling capabilities. Micro power relays are designed for low-current applications, ideal for systems requiring minimal energy consumption. Low power relays handle slightly higher loads, suitable for auxiliary functions. Medium power relays cater to moderate current applications, often used in control circuits. High power relays are built for heavy-duty tasks, managing significant current loads in critical automotive systems. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing vehicle functionality and efficiency.</p></p>
