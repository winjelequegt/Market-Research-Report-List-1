<p><p>Cable fault locating and test equipment encompasses various tools essential for identifying and diagnosing faults in electrical cables. Cable sheath fault locators detect insulation failures, while cable route tracers help trace the physical path of cables underground. Time domain reflectometers analyze signal reflections to pinpoint faults, and pin pointers provide precise location data for underground cables. Voltage surge generators apply a high-voltage surge to assess insulation integrity. Together, these tools enhance efficiency and reliability in cable maintenance and troubleshooting.</p></p>
