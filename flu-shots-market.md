<p><p>The flu shots market is categorized into three main types: split vaccines, subunit vaccines, and whole virus vaccines. Split vaccines contain fragmented virus particles, stimulating an immune response while minimizing side effects. Subunit vaccines include only specific protein components of the virus, offering targeted immunity. Whole virus vaccines use the complete inactivated virus, providing robust immunity but potentially more side effects. Each type serves to enhance protection against influenza, catering to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
