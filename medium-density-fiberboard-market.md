<p><p>The medium-density fiberboard (MDF) market is categorized into three main types: Fire-rated MDF, Moisture Resistant MDF, and General MDF. Fire-rated MDF is treated to resist flames and is ideal for applications in fire-sensitive environments. Moisture Resistant MDF is designed to withstand humidity, making it suitable for kitchens and bathrooms. General MDF, the most common type, is used for a wide range of applications including furniture and cabinetry due to its affordability and versatility. Each type meets specific performance requirements for distinct environments.</p></p>
