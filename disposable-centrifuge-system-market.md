<p><p>The Disposable Centrifuge System market comprises primarily two types: Filtration Centrifuges and Sedimentation Centrifuges. Filtration Centrifuges utilize filter media to separate solids from liquids, commonly employed in applications requiring high purity and efficiency. Sedimentation Centrifuges rely on the gravitational settling of particles to separate components, ideal for low solid concentrations. Together, these systems offer cost-effective, single-use solutions that enhance operational efficiency and reduce contamination risks in various industries, including pharmaceuticals and biotechnology.</p></p>
