<p><ul><li>Manual Lubrication Systems</li><li>Automatic Lubrication Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lubrication system market is divided into two main types: manual lubrication systems and automatic lubrication systems. Manual lubrication systems require operators to apply lubricant manually, typically through grease guns or oil cans, making them suitable for smaller machines or infrequent use. In contrast, automatic lubrication systems deliver precise amounts of lubricant at set intervals, enhancing efficiency and reducing wear in larger or more complex machinery. These systems improve maintenance practices, minimize downtime, and ensure optimal performance in industrial applications.</p></p>
