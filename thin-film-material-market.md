<p><ul><li>Chemical Deposition Process</li><li>Physical Deposition Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thin Film Material Market is primarily categorized into two types based on deposition processes: chemical and physical. The chemical deposition process involves applying thin layers using chemical reactions, enabling precise control over layer composition and thickness. Common methods include chemical vapor deposition (CVD) and sol-gel processes. In contrast, the physical deposition process relies on physical mechanisms such as evaporation or sputtering to create thin films, ensuring strong adhesion and uniformity. Both processes are crucial for applications in electronics, optoelectronics, and coatings.</p></p>
