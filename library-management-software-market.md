<p><p>Library Management Software (LMS) can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based LMS is hosted online, offering flexibility, accessibility from various devices, and automatic updates, making it ideal for institutions seeking cost-effective and scalable options. In contrast, on-premise LMS is installed locally on an institution's servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Both types aim to streamline library operations, enhance user experiences, and improve resource management.</p></p>
